Menu

Savory Mediterranean

Embark on a culinary journey to the sun-kissed Mediterranean shores with our mouthwatering dish. Succulent grilled chicken, marinated in a symphony of herbs and spices, served alongside a portion of roasted vegetables drizzled with olive oil. This vibrant plate promises an explosion of flavors that will transport your taste buds to the azure of Greece.

Exquisite Truffle Infusion

Indulge in the epitome of luxury with our exquisite truffle-infused creation. Perfectly al dente handmade pasta coated in a velvety, black truffle cream sauce, adorned with delicate shavings of premium truffles and Parmesan. This opulent dish is a testament to the artistry of fine dining and will leave you with a lingering sense of decadence.

Tantalizing Tandoori Feast

Prepare your palate for an explosion of authentic Indian flavors in our tantalizing tandoori feast. Juicy skewers of marinated chicken, lamb, and paneer, expertly grilled in a traditional clay oven, accompanied by fragrant basmati rice and freshly baked naan bread. The rich aroma of aromatic spices will transport you to the bustling streets of India.

Ocean's Bounty Paella

Dive into a delectable Spanish tradition with our Ocean's Bounty Paella. A vibrant saffron-infused rice paella adorned with an abundance of succulent seafood, including shrimp, mussels, clams, and calamari, all simmered to perfection with a medley of bell peppers and peas. This flavorful dish captures the essence of coastal Spain in every savory bite.

Heirloom Tomato Caprese

Celebrate the beauty of simplicity with our Heirloom Tomato Caprese. Juicy slices of colorful heirloom tomatoes layered with velvety buffalo mozzarella, drizzled with aged balsamic reduction, and garnished with fragrant basil leaves. This light and refreshing plate are a delightful ode to Italian classics.

Chocolate Indulgence

Give in to your sweet temptations with our Decadent Chocolate Indulgence. A symphony of chocolate delights awaits – rich chocolate lava cake oozing with molten chocolate, accompanied by a velvety scoop of artisanal chocolate gelato and topped with a sprinkling of toasted hazelnuts. This dessert extravaganza is a chocolate lover's dream come true.
